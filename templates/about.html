Sometimes I wonder if I am making the right life choices. Especially
those decisions that have grave impact on where they will lead me.
How do I know the still voice of the Devine versus those of my own
telling me what to do and not to do. Like, I know I enjoy aspects of
computing which are more practical than theoretical; more like a technician.
Yet the more qualified persons tend to do the supervision of the
technician which to me takes the fun out of the practicality that I enjoy.
